LCOE Dashboard

Enter CAPEX and OPEX on a EUR/MW basis, installed capacity in MW, financing assumptions, year 1 yield per MW, annual degradation, annual OPEX inflation, and plant life. The dashboard calculates total project costs, annual energy output, escalated OPEX, and the discounted LCOE.

Formula: LCOE = PV(Costs) / PV(Energy)
